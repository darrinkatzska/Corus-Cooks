Bucket o' Salad:

carrots
celery
cucumber
zucchini
sweet bell peppers
radishes
broccoli florets
chickpeas or red kidney beans

On the side (added separately each day):

quinoa (cooked in broth)
sliced white mushrooms
avocados and/or feta
sauerkraut

Dressing

1 tbsp dijon mustard
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1/4 cup white miso paste
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/2 cup flax oil
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
salt to taste
